5.5. The Waveforms Menu

Waveforms->Time Frame (T)

Enables or disables the time frame mode, where waveforms are selected only in a certain time frame rather than evenly throughout the entire recording

Waveforms->Overlay (O)

Enables or disables the overlay mode, where the waveforms for different clusters are displayed on top of one another rather than side-by-side

Waveforms->Mean and Standard Deviation (M)

Enables or disables the statistics mode, where the mean waveform and the standard deviation are displayed rather than individual waveforms

Waveforms->Increase Amplitude (I)

Increases the vertical scale

Waveforms->Decrease Amplitude (D)

Decreases the vertical scale
