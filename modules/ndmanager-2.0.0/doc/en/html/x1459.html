5.7. The Traces Menu

Traces->Increase Channel Amplitudes (Ctrl-Shift-I)

Increases the channel amplitudes

Traces->Decrease Channel Amplitudes (Ctrl-Shift-D)

Decreases the channel amplitudes

Traces->Show Labels (Ctrl-L)

Shows or hides the labels (channel IDs and gains).
