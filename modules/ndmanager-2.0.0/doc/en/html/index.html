NDManager (Neurophysiological Data Manager) is a simple graphical application designed to help neurophysiologists manage their experimental recording parameters (e.g., number of channels and sampling rate of the acquisition system) and process their data (data filtering, spike extraction, PCA, etc.)

NDManager was developed by Lynn Hazan in G. Buzsáki's lab (CMBN, Rutgers Newark, USA). The NDManager Plugins were developed by Michaël Zugaro (CNRS-Collège de France, France). If you use Klusters for your analyses, please be kind enough to cite the following article in your publications:

L. Hazan, M. Zugaro, G. Buzsáki (2006). Klusters, NeuroScope, NDManager: a Free Software Suite for Neurophysiological Data Processing and Visualization, J. Neurosci. Methods 155:207-216.
